My Stories


Bass Guitar

When I first practiced the bass guitar, fingering was difficult and the sound wasn't clear.

After practicing for an hour every day, I improved a lot and now I can play effortlessly. Through this experience, I learned the importance of tenacity and consistency.

Traits I developed: Tenacity / Consistency


Group Project

During a team project, there was a conflict of opinions among teammates. However, we communicated, coordinated our ideas, and completed the project successfully.

I learned that communication and respect among team members are essential for successful collaboration.

Traits I developed: Communication Skills / Respect for Others


Art

When I first tried to draw, I started without any basic knowledge about drawing.

The result was poor, and I realized the importance of learning the basics before starting. After that, I studied human face ratios and now I can draw faces more easily.

Traits I developed: Creativity / Willingness to Learn the Basics
